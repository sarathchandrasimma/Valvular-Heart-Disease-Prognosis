This application uses a real-world dataset collected from Medicore Hospital, Srikakulam consisting of 1280 patient records with 13 clinical features to train a hybrid machine learning model that predicts the risk of heart disease.

üß¨ Key Dataset Characteristics:

  • Total Samples: 1280
  • Target Variable: Indicates presence (1) or absence (0) of heart disease
  • Source: Medicore Hospital, Srikakulam - Real clinical data
  • Clinical Features Include:
    • AGE - Patient age in years
    • GENDER - Male (1) or Female (0)
    • AORTIC VALVE - Aortic valve condition
    • LEFT ATRIUM - Left atrium size (cm)
    • EDD - End Diastolic Dimension
    • ESD - End Systolic Dimension
    • EF - Ejection Fraction (%)
    • IVS (D) - Interventricular Septum Thickness
    • PW (D) - Posterior Wall Thickness
    • AORTA - Aortic root diameter
    • I.A.S - Interatrial Septum
    • RVSP - Right Ventricular Systolic Pressure
    • RWMA - Regional Wall Motion Abnormality
